Project deep dive

Glossary: A Language-Learning Chrome Extension

A thesis-driven product concept for helping intermediate to advanced language learners understand slang, culturally embedded expressions, authentic usage, and spoken-language nuance while watching streaming media.

Product landing page Open live demo View full thesis PDF

Thesis summary

The project responds to a gap between school-based language learning and real-life communication. Through learner interviews, expert interviews, a landscape audit, survey research, prototype design, and testing, the thesis argues that many learners can pass exams yet still struggle with slang, idioms, humor, cultural references, etiquette, and natural conversational choices.

Glossary turns streaming content into a low-friction learning environment. Instead of asking learners to set aside extra study time, it embeds vocabulary support, cultural notes, AI sentence explanations, pronunciation, replay, review, and speaking practice into media habits learners already have.

Product context

Audience
Intermediate, upper-intermediate, and advanced learners, especially international students.
Core problem
Textbook language often does not prepare learners for everyday speech, slang, humor, and cultural nuance.
Design medium
Chrome extension for streaming services, chosen for authentic input, learner-paced viewing, and habit integration.
Validation
Concept and usability testing with 5 testers, with an average idea score of 83.6/100.

Extension features

  1. Dual subtitles Display target-language and native-language subtitles together, with furigana support for Japanese.
  2. Built-in word hints Show lightweight hints for unfamiliar words, slang, and phrases directly in the subtitles.
  3. Hover popup cards Reveal meaning, example sentence, translation, and note-taking space when learners hover over a word.
  4. Pronunciation playback Let learners play a word's pronunciation repeatedly from the subtitle or popup card.
  5. AI sentence breakdown Explain sentence structure, key phrases, grammar, nuance, and relevant cultural context with AI.
  6. Cultural notes and pragmatic usage tips Explain cultural references, etiquette, jokes, sarcasm, pop culture, and how to respond naturally.
  7. Colloquial expression and idiom support Explain slang, idioms, and expressions in context, with examples and follow-up AI questions.
  8. Sentence-by-sentence pause mode Auto-pause after each subtitle line so learners can shadow, repeat, or check comprehension.
  9. Loop playback Let learners mark a start and end point, then replay that video segment repeatedly.
  10. Single-sentence replay Replay the current subtitle sentence instantly when learners miss auditory details.
  11. Proficiency-level video recommendations Recommend videos matched to the learner's level after an initial placement flow.
  12. Vocabulary tracking and review quizzes Automatically save looked-up words, support notes, resurface vocabulary, and quiz missed items later.
  13. AI-based speaking practice Offer role dialogues or Q&A based on the video, allowing multilingual clarification when needed.
  14. Instant AI speaking feedback Give feedback on pronunciation, word choice, and speaking performance to prepare for real conversations.

Testing insights

Testers responded positively to the clear interface, customizable subtitles and word hints, AI breakdowns, cultural explanations, replay and looping controls, shadowing mode, and the overall feeling of learning smoothly without leaving the streaming experience.

  • Average concept rating: 83.6/100.
  • 60% projected adoption among testers.
  • Strongest perceived value: slang, idioms, cultural knowledge, contextual understanding, and authentic usage.

Iteration after feedback

  • Shortened AI breakdowns and made them more structured.
  • Added function hints for discoverability.
  • Renamed and redesigned the hint-level slider.
  • Revised popup cards with speaker/save icons, clearer examples, translations, and notes.
  • Added cultural-notes display modes: disabled, hover to reveal, and always expanded.
  • Added usage/currentness context for slang and expressions.
  • Added full transcript view with optional translation.
  • Added folders and color categorization for saved vocabulary.
